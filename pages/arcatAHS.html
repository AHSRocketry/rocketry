ARC at AHS

The American Rocketry Challenge (ARC) is an international high-school competition for student rocketeers to fly a rocket to meet very stringent requirements, such as carrying eggs, reaching a target height and flight time, and other limitations that make the ARC so rewarding. Students compete with 1,000 teams across the country for a $20,000 prize.

Find out more at rocketcontest.org
