OpenRocket is open source software used by rocketeers to design and test a model rocket before actually building and flying it. The software accurately computes the aerodynamic properties of rockets and simulates their flight, returning a wide range of technical results, ensuring that a rocket design is safe and viable.

The program can be divided into two sections: Rocket Designwhere you design the model rocket, and Flight Simulationwhere you run simulations with a variety of motors to get an idea

This page will teach you to design a basic rocket using OpenRocket. Read through the entire page or jump to various sections of the rocket by clicking picture of the coinciding rocket design below.

 

To be successful, each rocket must have the following elements.

  1. Nose Cone
  2. Body Tube
  3. Fins
  4. Motor Mount, containing:
    • Motor Tube
    • centering rings
    • hook/retainer
    • motor block
  5. launch lug
  6. shock cord
  7. parachute or streamer
