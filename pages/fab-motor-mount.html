Fabricating Motor Mount Assemblies

Motor Mount Materials

Gather the items below in order to build a motor mount. To read more about designing a motor mount, go here.

  • Motor Tube
  • Motor Block
  • Retaining Hook or Ring
  • Centering Rings, at least two
  • Shock Cord

Steps to Building a Motor Mount

  1. Measure and mark location of the centering rings using the information from the OpenRocket Model. To find this information, open each centering ring and look at the "Placement" section. The "Position relative to:" box tells you the position the placement is measured from; the plus indicates how far the ring is from this position. A negative value moves the ring to the left and a positive value moves it to the right.
