Design & Build — Start Here

Follow the steps below. Click any item to open the detailed module in this panel.

1) Mission Requirements & Constraints

Define your rocket’s goal (basic flight, payload, altitude) and inventory materials, motors, and tools.

2) Design in OpenRocket

Create the digital design and verify stability (CG vs CP), then select an appropriate motor.

3) Draft Parts in OnShape

Create CAD for printed and laser-cut parts (nose, transitions, rings, fins, retainers, jigs).

4) Fabrication — 3D Printing

Export STL from OnShape, slice in Cura, then print nose cones, transitions, and retainers.
