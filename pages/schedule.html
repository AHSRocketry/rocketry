            AHS Rocket Club

            AHS Rocket Club meets to push the boundaries of our rocketeering beyond the time limits of ELW week. We meet weekly on Tuesdays between 3:30 and 5:00. We are working toward competing in the national ARC competition as well as our own personal projects. $50 tuition to join to allow for purchase of supplies.

            Utah Rocket Club (UROC) Launches

            All rocketry students are welcome to attend extra launch days with UROC, the local NAR and Tripoli chapter in Utah. Anyone is welcome to come to these launches to either fly or just watch. The launches take place at Frank Hunt Field, start at 8:00 AM (some people camp out overnight), and last as long as there are rocketeers wanting to fly. Often there will be upwards of 200 rocketeers on the field. It is an excellent time to meet new people and learn new things about rocketry. You can also earn your L1 and L2 certifications at these launches. Find out more at https://uroc.org/
